THE ARTIST

Zainab Huseni Jabla

I was about 6 years old when I first began painting. I used to spoil all of my mothers paints and fill the empty bottles with water.
This was my usual past time when my parents went out or by any chance I was alone at home. I painted the carpets, curtains and my school books.
Finally my mom decided to put into a painting class and that’s when my journey began.

I was fast in learning the techniques and finished the course in 1 month which usually took a month and a half. In this class I began with acrylic paints and soon after
I was done with this course I bought a few cheap oil paints. I was always attracted towards oil colors.Later I decided to join an oil painting class.That’s how my journey
truly began!
